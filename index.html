About Me

I'm an experienced IT professional and SaaS founder with a background delivering technology solutions across industries including automotive, sports, entertainment, finance, and healthcare. My work sits at the intersection of hands-on technical execution and business problem-solving, with a focus on building systems that are reliable, scalable, and easy to use.

Throughout my career, I've partnered with organizations to drive digital transformation, optimize operations, and implement solutions that deliver measurable business value. My experience spans consulting, full-stack development, product design, and strategic technology leadership.

Outside of enterprise IT, I founded RacquetSuite to solve a real operational gap in a niche, underserved market. Building the platform end-to-end allowed me to apply the same principles I use in enterprise environments, including process optimization, automation, security, and user-centric design, while owning the full product lifecycle from concept to production.
